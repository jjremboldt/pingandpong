Play "Math" Ping-Pong

Play by entering any number the game instantly calculates:

  1. Numbers that are "divisible by 3" show a "ping".
  2. Numbers that are "divisible by 5" show a "pong".
  3. Numbers that are "divisible by 3 and 5" get a "ping-pong".
